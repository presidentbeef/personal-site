Stuff about me

My name is Justin.

I've lived in Ohio and Kentucky (both briefly), Montana, Washington, and California.

I have gone to school at Seattle University and University of California, Los Angeles. I am still at UCLA in the computer science PhD program, possibly forever.

I have worked at Klir Technologies, AT&T Interactive, and am now at Twitter. I also did some other stuff in between.

I have visited Canada, Israel, Germany, the Czech Republic, Austria, and Australia.

I like writing programs, shooting arrows, and hanging out with my wife.

My first computer was a TRS-80 Model 100 my uncle gave me when I was ~11. Model 100 BASIC was my introduction to programming. When it was time to go to college, I just looked up what major included "programming" and went for it. When it was time to graduate with a BS in CS, I decided I wasn't done yet so I went off to get a graduate degree in CS and there I still am.
