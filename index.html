Stuff about me

My name is Justin.

I have lived in Ohio and Kentucky (both briefly), Montana, Washington, and California.

I received my BS in computer science from Seattle University and my PhD in computer science from University of California, Los Angeles.

I have worked at Klir Technologies, AT&T Interactive, Twitter, and am now at SurveyMonkey. I also did some other stuff in between.

I like writing programs, shooting arrows, and hanging out with my wife.
